about chanki
According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Chanki village is 251623. Chanki village is located in Nokha Tehsil of Rohtas district in Bihar, India. It is situated 20km away from sub-district headquarter Nokha and 30km away from district headquarter Sasaram. As per 2009 stats, Chanki village is also a gram panchayat. The total geographical area of village is 185 hectares. Chanki has a total population of 2,075 peoples. There are about 289 houses in Chanki village. Nokha is nearest town to Chanki which is approximately 20km away.
